Products

White-label Platform - Thrifty.com

As a Product Manager at Hertz, I spearheaded the development and launch of a white-label shop and book platform for the Thrifty brand. This innovative platform was designed to streamline and unify the online booking process across all Hertz brands, replacing the need for multiple websites and regional variations.

The platform's key strength lies in its ability to be seamlessly integrated into any containing website and internationalized, thereby enabling rapid scaling of improvements across brands and regions. This strategic initiative not only reduced tech debt and maintenance but also significantly enhanced user experience.
