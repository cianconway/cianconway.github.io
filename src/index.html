Products

White-label Booking Platform - Thrifty.com

As a Product Manager at Hertz, I spearheaded the development and launch of a white-label shop and book platform for the Thrifty brand. This innovative platform was designed to streamline and unify the online booking process across all Hertz brands, replacing the need for multiple websites and regional variations.

The problem was customers were not progressing through the booking flow and were instead dropping off at various stages. This was due to a number of factors including poor UX, and a lack of consistency across brands and regions.

Post-launch, the platform demonstrated its value by increasing controlled conversion by 30%, contributing to an estimated annualised revenue of $30 million for all brands.

White-label Modify & Cancel

As a Product Manager at Hertz, I launched a white-label modify and cancel platform for the Thrifty brand. This was designed to allow customers to modify or cancel their bookings made on the white-label platform.

The problem was customers were not able to modify or cancel their bookings on the existing platform and as such were contacting customer support directly to modify and cancel their bookings.

Post-launch, the platform demonstrated its value by reducing customer support calls related to modification for Thrifty by 15%.

Electric Vehicle Trip Planner

The EV trip planner was built for Hertz and included both android and iOS apps as well as the website. The platform was designed to allow customers to plan their trip in an electric vehicle both in the bookning flow on the vehicle selection screen and on the my rentals section.

Hertz are moving towards and electric vehicle based fleet. The problem we had identified was customers were not booking electric vehicles as much and it was determined that this was due to range anxiety and EVs being a newer technology that customers were unfamiliar with.

Post-launch, we saw a signifcant uptick in customers booking electric vehicles.
